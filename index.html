Custom seed

Any text is a valid scene. Shared and custom seeds run as Exhibition — fully playable, but they never move your bankroll.

Difficulty

  1. Watch. Every scene is ten seconds from a crash. The tell is hidden in plain sight — a drifting van, a light about to change.
  2. Bet. Tap markets to build a slip: who crashes, who walks away, what gets hit. Chain picks into a parlay for bigger odds.
  3. Lock. At the incident the clock stops so you can study the freeze — then press BET and time runs again. Above level 7 there is no freeze.
  4. Settle. Physics decides. Chips turn green the instant they land, and the bank pays out at rest.

Bust at $0 and the bank stakes you a fresh $100. Vehicles and property only — nobody is ever in these cars.
